Wonderful Morning

How come you don't enjoy this wonderful morning?

When the sun is lighting up the sky outside, it's daytime. Most adults are at work during the daytime and come home at nighttime.

While daytime is defined as "after sunrise and before sunset," it describes this time of day even on dark, gray, days, or in parts of the world that get limited sunlight during the winter. A more accurate way to think of daytime is the hours after you wake up in the morning and before you start to think about going to bed at night. When it's daytime where you are, it's nighttime on the opposite hemisphere of the Earth.

Morning

Morning is the period of time from sunrise to noon. Morning can be defined as starting from midnight to noon, or from sunrise to noon, or from when one wakes up to noon.

Afternoon

Afternoon is a time when the sun is descending from its daytime peak. During the afternoon, the sun moves from roughly the center of the sky to deep in the west.

Night

Night is the period of ambient darkness from sunset to sunrise in each twenty-four hours. It is usually considered to start around 7 PM and to last to about 5 AM.
